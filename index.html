The Photographer

Benazir

Food has always been how my family spoke — across languages, borders, and time zones. I grew up watching the way a meal could hold an entire history, and somewhere along the way I started pointing a camera at it.

I'm based in Rochester, Minnesota. I shoot food, drink, and product — but what I'm really after is the feeling a dish carries. The specific orange of turmeric. The weight of a ceramic bowl. The light that only exists at that table, at that moment.

I'm part-time by choice. I take on a small number of projects, ones where the brief has some breathing room, where we can push the image further than a shot list. Ones where there's space to collaborate, experiment, and arrive somewhere unexpected. If you're building something and you want images that actually mean something, let's figure out if we're a good fit.
